BetterView Glasses

For my Intro to Engineering Design course, I worked with a team of 4 to design a pair of assistive glasses for the visually impaired.

The frames were modeled in Fusion360 and 3D printed. Ultrasonic sensors were then mounted and used to detect the distance and angle of of in front of the user. This information was then transmitted to a pair of Bluetooth headphones. I wrote the Arduino code (similar to C++) for the ultrasonic sensors and the Bluetooth module.

Card Reader Classifier

Oloid is a contactless-tracing startup based in the Silicon Valley. As a software intern this past summer, I was tasked with creating a RFID card reader classifier for easily identifying the specific make and model of a badge scanner.

I creating a Tensorflow pipeline with dropout regularization to reduce overfitting. I also used data autotuning and data augmentation to improve the model's accuracy. I then used React.js to create a front-end web application that allows users to upload images of RFID card readers and receive a prediction in real-time.
